Closing entry

Thank you for being part of it.

Nexor started as a bet that AI voice agents were worth building seriously, one person, one client at a time. Everything in this archive — every proposal, every demo, every late revision — exists because someone was willing to say yes before there was a track record to point to. That's the part of this I keep. The business is closed, but I'm grateful for every conversation, every client, and everyone who helped along the way.
